Our Speakers

Thinkers. Doers. Idea-generators. TEDx VJTI felt extremely honored for being a platform to their Ideas to fly!.

"Imagine if our freedom fighters were afraid of fighting, then?"

Dipesh Tank

An activist on a mission to keep Mumbai's female commuters safe during their journey in local train in James Bond style. This good samaritan's message and initiatives are making the difference. Be the difference.



"Sunil was about to quit his education because he did not know English"

Sunil Khandbahale

Innovator, Entrepreneur, an MIT Sloan fellow and the Founder and CEO of khandbahale.com, a free multilingual digital dictionary and translation platform for 23 languages, used by over 100 million users worldwide.



"Life is either a daring adventure or nothing"

Shital Mahajan

A Padmashree Awardee and successful skydiver who broke 6 World and 17 National records. She overcame the nuisances of society to reach the sky and proved that for dreamers and believers sky is not a limit.



"Chase down your passion like it's the last bus of the night"

The Spin Doctor

From being a practising doctor to a DJ in a pre-eminent band not everyone has a story like this to apprise. This man has established himself as a premier DJ (Turntablist), who spins music that keeps people on the dance floor.
